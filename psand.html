P-Sand (Plastering Sand)

P-Sand (Plastering Sand) is a fine-grade manufactured sand specifically designed for plastering applications. Known for its smooth texture and consistent grain size, P-Sand provides an excellent finish and strong bonding for interior and exterior wall plastering.

✔ Features of High-Quality P-Sand

  • Very fine and smooth particles
  • Ideal for wall and ceiling plastering
  • Zero organic impurities
  • Ensures crack-free and even finishing
  • Better water retention and workability

✔ Applications

  • Exterior wall plastering
  • Interior smooth plastering
  • Ceiling plaster work
  • Tile laying base preparation
  • Finishing coats and skim coats

✔ Why Choose P-Sand?

P-Sand offers maximum smoothness, better binding, and a superior surface finish compared to river sand. Manufactured with precise grading, it prevents cracks, improves durability, and ensures a premium plastering quality for all construction projects.
